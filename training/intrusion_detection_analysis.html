Intrusion Detection Analysis

Interested ? Contact Us

Intrusion is entering another’s behavior and property and communication without permission. Corporates suffer a lot of attacks and loss due to a cyber criminal's intrusion, although they have got end line security products. There are many ways for mitigating the intrusions. But these remediations are post methods. The best way is to train the staff, so that they can take counter measures in case of emergency.

Kubotor brings you a training “Intrusion Detection Analysis” of 2 days for corporate sectors. In this 2 day training, we will train the employees and staff the various ways of how an attacker can intrude your system and network, with live-scenario demonstrations. We will also train the various ways of preventing those intrusion and analysis for the same, which includes secure system configuration and other configurational audits.

Once the training program is completed, candidates will be awarded with :
