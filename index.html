Who We Are

Kubotor (cube-o-tor) does not stand for an individual, rather it is a group of cyber security enthusiasts. We have a motive of “Promoting Cyber Security Awareness and to Boost Security of Individuals and Organisations”. With a Hands-on Approach, we always try to make every aspect possible. Gathering Cyber Security Enthusiasts and creating an Elite Community is one of the motive of this organisation.

What We Do

In this unaware world, we try to make people aware about Cyber Space’s benefits and consequences, for those who do not know. Our services are neither limited nor restricted to any specific domain. We provide training, seminars, workshops and Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing services. We train people having keen interest and are eager to inculcate the knowledge of cyber. We make a professionally expert person out of those who show us interest.
